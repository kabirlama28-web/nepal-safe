What We Do

Everything You Need in a Crisis

Nepal Safe brings critical tools under one roof so responders and citizens act fast when seconds matter.

🗺️

Live Disaster Mapping

Real-time maps showing affected zones, shelter locations, and blocked roads across all 77 districts.

📡

Emergency Alerts

Instant SMS and in-app alerts to registered volunteers and first responders when a new event is reported.

🤝

Volunteer Coordination

Connect NGOs, government teams, and citizen volunteers on a shared digital platform during relief operations.

📦

Resource Tracking

Track food, medical supplies, and equipment from distribution centres to affected communities.

📷

Disaster Documentation

Geo-tagged photos and field reports help agencies assess damage and plan targeted aid delivery.

📚

Preparedness Training

Free guides and training resources to help communities prepare before disaster strikes.
