Who We Are

About Nepal Safe

A government-backed digital platform dedicated to saving lives through coordinated disaster response across Nepal.

Our Mission

Built for Nepal's Most Vulnerable Moments

Nepal sits in one of the world's most disaster-prone regions — earthquakes, floods, landslides, and avalanches affect millions of people every year. Nepal Safe was created to bridge the communication gap between citizens, first responders, and government agencies during these critical events.

We believe faster coordination means fewer lives lost. Our platform gives every Nepali — from Himalayan village to Terai plains — access to real-time disaster data, emergency contacts, and community support networks.

Our Journey

History of Nepal Safe

2015

Founded After Gorkha Earthquake

The 7.8 magnitude earthquake exposed critical coordination gaps. Nepal Safe was conceptualised by a group of disaster management experts and IT professionals in Kathmandu.

2017

Platform Launch — Beta

First version launched covering 10 high-risk districts, integrating with Nepal Police and Armed Police Force alert systems.

2020

National Expansion

Extended to all 77 districts. Partnered with Ministry of Home Affairs and NDRRMA for official endorsement.

2024

50,000 Users Milestone

Reached 50,000 registered users — volunteers, first responders, government officials, and citizens — marking Nepal Safe as the country's primary disaster coordination platform.

2026

AI-Powered Early Warning

Launched machine-learning based flood and landslide early warning system in partnership with DHM and satellite data providers.
